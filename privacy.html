Ascesa Vow — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23/05/2026

Welcome to Ascesa Vow, operated by Kass Studiolo ("Company").

Ascesa Vow is a self-improvement and habit accountability application designed to help users create personal commitments, track habits, build routines, and monitor progress through motivational and symbolic experiences.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Ascesa Vow mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide and improve the Service.

Account Information

  • Email address.
  • Authentication provider information (Google, Apple ID).
  • User profile information.
  • Profile images uploaded by users.

User Content

  • Habit and restriction entries.
  • Personal goals and commitments.
  • Progress tracking information.
  • Check-ins and streak data.
  • Self-assessment responses.
  • Notes, reviews, and user feedback.

Notification & Reminder Information

  • Reminder preferences.
  • Scheduled notification settings.
  • Push notification tokens.

Payment Information

Payments and subscriptions may be processed through:

  • Apple In-App Purchases.
  • Google Play Billing.
  • Stripe.
  • PayPal (if enabled).

We do not directly store full payment card details.

Analytics & Technical Data

We may collect:

  • Device type.
  • Operating system.
  • App version.
  • Crash reports.
  • Usage analytics.
  • General geographic region information.
  • Interaction and feature usage statistics.

This information may be collected through:

  • Firebase Analytics.
  • Google Analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service.
  • Maintain user accounts.
  • Synchronize contracts and progress.
  • Send reminders and notifications.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Monitor app performance and stability.
  • Process subscriptions and purchases.
  • Prevent abuse or misuse of the platform.
  • Respond to support requests.

3. Data Access and Review

We respect the privacy of your personal content.

We do not routinely review or monitor user content or personal activity data. Access to user information is limited to situations where it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide the Service.

  • Respond to a user support request.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the platform.
  • Investigate abuse, fraud, or unlawful activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Ascesa Vow may send:

8. Data Retention

We retain user information only as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Resolve disputes.
  • Enforce agreements.

When accounts are deleted, associated user data is scheduled for deletion within approximately 30 days unless retention is required by law.

9. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion directly from within the application.

When an account deletion request is submitted:

  • Associated contracts and personal data are scheduled for deletion.
  • Deletion is typically completed within 30 days.

Some limited information may be retained temporarily where required for:

  • Legal compliance.
  • Fraud prevention.
  • Financial recordkeeping.
  • Security purposes.

For assistance regarding account deletion, users may contact: support@ascesavow.app
