# Ascesa Vow — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 23/05/2026

Welcome to Ascesa Vow, operated by Kass Studiolo ("Company").

Ascesa Vow is a self-improvement and habit accountability application designed to help users create personal commitments, track habits, build routines, and monitor progress through motivational and symbolic experiences.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Ascesa Vow mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide and improve the Service.

### Account Information
- Email address.
- Authentication provider information (Google, Apple ID).
- User profile information.
- Profile images uploaded by users.

### User Content
- Habit and restriction entries.
- Personal goals and commitments.
- Progress tracking information.
- Check-ins and streak data.
- Self-assessment responses.
- Notes, reviews, and user feedback.

### Notification & Reminder Information
- Reminder preferences.
- Scheduled notification settings.
- Push notification tokens.

### Payment Information
Payments and subscriptions may be processed through:
- Apple In-App Purchases.
- Google Play Billing.
- Stripe.
- PayPal (if enabled).

We do not directly store full payment card details.

### Analytics & Technical Data
We may collect:
- Device type.
- Operating system.
- App version.
- Crash reports.
- Usage analytics.
- General geographic region information.
- Interaction and feature usage statistics.

This information may be collected through:
- Firebase Analytics.
- Google Analytics.
- Firebase Crashlytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:
- Provide and operate the Service.
